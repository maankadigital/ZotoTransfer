

MyTransfer
Brand Strategy & Product Requirements Document (PRD)
## Executive Summary
MyTransfer is a modern, secure, and lightning-fast file transfer platform that enables individuals and
businesses to upload, share, and transfer large files effortlessly.
The platform is designed to eliminate the limitations of email attachments and complicated file-sharing
tools by providing a simple, reliable, and secure experience.
Users can upload files, generate download links, send files via email, track downloads, and manage
transfers from a centralized dashboard.
